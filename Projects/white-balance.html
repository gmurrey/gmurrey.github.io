White Balance

How It Works

White Balance is the basis of color in a photograph. Depending on the setting a photo is taken in, the color white will appear different. White balance is measured in Kelvin or K (i.e. 4000 K, 5000 K, etc.). White balance is what gives a difffferent hue and tone to imagery.
